## ğŸ’¡ Understanding Your Results

### Body Shapes

**Hourglass** - Balanced shoulders and hips, defined waist
- Best: Fitted styles, belts, wrap dresses
- Avoid: Baggy, shapeless clothes

**Pear** - Hips wider than shoulders
- Best: Bright tops, dark bottoms, A-line styles
- Avoid: Tight light-colored pants

**Apple** - Broader shoulders, undefined waist
- Best: V-necks, flowy tops, empire waist
- Avoid: Tight belts, crop tops

**Rectangle** - Similar width shoulders, waist, and hips
- Best: Ruffles, belts, layered looks
- Avoid: Straight, boxy cuts

**Inverted Triangle** - Shoulders wider than hips
- Best: Simple tops, wide-leg pants
- Avoid: Shoulder pads, boat necks

### Face Shapes

**Oval** - Balanced proportions
- Most necklines work!

**Round** - Soft angles
- Best: V-necks, long earrings

**Square** - Strong jawline
- Best: Scoop necks, hoop earrings

**Heart** - Wide forehead, narrow chin
- Best: Boat necks, off-shoulder

**Diamond** - High cheekbones
- Best: Strapless, scoop necks

### Skin Tones

**Warm** - Golden undertones
- Colors: Mustard, Olive Green, Coral, Warm Red

**Cool** - Pink/blue undertones
- Colors: Royal Blue, Emerald Green, Purple

**Neutral** - Mix of undertones
- Colors: Dusty Pink, Jade Green, Peach
